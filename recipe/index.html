Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

A peanut butter and jelly sandwich, or PB&J, includes one or more layers of peanut butter and one or more layers of either jelly or jam on bread.

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 tablespoons of peanut butter
  • 2 tablespoons of jelly or jam

Directions:

  1. Spread peanut butter on one slice of bread.
  2. Spread jelly or jam on the other slice of bread.
  3. Press both slices of bread together.
  4. Cut in half diagonally (optional).
